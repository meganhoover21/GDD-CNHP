30 Year Climate Trends in CO, NE, SD, and WY

Organization: CNHP Affiliates: Megan Hoover, Andrew Zimbelman, Chippy Marx

We downloaded monthly and daily climate data from https://prism.oregonstate.edu/ based off of the species and locations we provided. We are comparing 1991-2020 monthly precipitation and temperature trends to 2026 trends. These species are located in the Rocky Mountain eco region.

Pixelated heat map of the 30 year temperature trends compared to 2026

This shows the raw values for each month of each year.

Pixelated heat map of the temperature anomaly across species from 1991-2020

Anomalies are compared to a baseline value and they remove seasonality so you can compare across time more meaningfully.

Blue values indicate lower temps compared to the month of the previous year and red values indicate that the month was warmer than the previous years.

Pixelated precipitation map of the 30 year trends compared to 2026

This shows the raw mean precipitation values month by month per year.

Pixelated Precipitation anomaly map of the plant species from 1991-2020

This map shows the difference in precipitation of each month compared to each year. Blue values indicate that there was more precipitation, while red values indicate that there was lower precipitation.
